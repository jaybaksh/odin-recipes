Thai curry to warm your heart and soul

This warming curry is absolutely gorgeous any day of the week.

Ingridients

  • 2 tablespoons Thai red or green curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 pound (450g) protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu), diced
  • 1 cup assorted vegetables (bell peppers, bamboo shoots, baby corn)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (or soy sauce for a vegetarian option)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Fresh basil or cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the curry paste and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk, stirring to combine with the curry paste.
  4. Add the diced protein and cook until it's almost done.
  5. Stir in the assorted vegetables and cook until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Season the curry with fish sauce and sugar, adjusting to taste.
  7. Simmer for a few more minutes until everything is cooked through and flavors are well combined.
  8. Serve the Thai curry over cooked rice.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil or cilantro.
  10. Enjoy your delicious homemade Thai curry!
