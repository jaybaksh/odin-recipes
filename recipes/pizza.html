GOAT Pizza Recipe: The Greatest Pizza recipe of all time

This recipe is one of my favourites of all time. Super quick and easy to make at home.

Ingridients

You'll need this Ingridients to make this badass pizza!

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature for your pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings, distributing them evenly.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for flavor.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven according to the pizza dough instructions or until the crust is golden and the
  9. cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
